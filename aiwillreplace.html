🤖 "AI Will Replace All Jobs" — Not Entirely True

AI is transforming industries, but that doesn’t mean all jobs are going away.

  • 🔄 AI is automating repetitive tasks, not creative or emotional work.
  • 🧠 Roles that involve strategy, creativity, and empathy are still human-driven.
  • 📈 AI will create new jobs in development, ethics, management, and more.

The future is AI + Humans working together — not one replacing the other.
